# Privacy Policy
Effective date: February 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Quiznetic collects, uses, and stores information when you use the app.

## 1. Information We Collect

- Account information from Firebase Authentication:
  - user ID (UID)
  - email address (if provided by your sign-in method)
  - display name (if provided by your sign-in method)
  - anonymous account status for guest users
- Gameplay and score data:
  - category, difficulty, score, and timestamps
  - leaderboard entries and rank-related fields
  - score attempt identifiers for idempotent submission
- Basic technical metadata required by Firebase services for security and reliability.

## 2. How We Use Information

- To authenticate users and maintain guest/account sessions.
- To save and sync scores, attempts, and profile progress.
- To display leaderboard results.
- To protect score integrity and reduce abuse.
- To operate, troubleshoot, and improve app reliability.

## 3. Data Storage And Providers

- Data is stored in Firebase services (Google Cloud) used by Quiznetic:
  - Firebase Authentication
  - Cloud Firestore
- Data may be processed in regions selected by project configuration.

## 4. Anonymous Guest Accounts

- You can use Quiznetic as a guest.
- Guest data is tied to your anonymous Firebase UID.
- If you upgrade your account, data may remain associated with the same UID.

## 5. Data Sharing

- We do not sell your personal data.
- Leaderboard data is visible to authenticated users of the app.
- We may share data with service providers only as needed to run Quiznetic.
- We may disclose data when required by law or to enforce app security.

## 6. Data Retention

- We keep account and gameplay data while needed for app operation, security, and legitimate business purposes.
- We may retain limited records when required by law or to resolve disputes.

## 7. Your Choices

- You can stop using the app at any time.
- You can request account/data deletion support by contacting `support@quiznetic.app`.
- If you uninstall before account linking, anonymous data recovery may not be possible.

## 8. Children

- Quiznetic is not directed to children under the minimum age required by applicable law in your region.

## 9. Security

- We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards, but no method of storage or transmission is guaranteed to be 100% secure.

## 10. Policy Updates

- We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
- Updated versions become effective when published in the app/repository.

## 11. Contact

- Privacy questions: `support@quiznetic.app`
